# SAFE-FAST Project Phone Q&A Clarifications

## Direct Q&A

Question: Do we have a trade plan or a setup-recognition system?

Answer: SAFE-FAST is still being turned into a full trade plan. It has recognition and discipline components, but completeness gates are required before results can count as trade-plan proof.

Question: What creates fake confidence?

Answer: Counting results before contract, entry, exit, cost, liquidity, fill, and invalidation rules are fixed.

Question: Do we likely have the raw data source?

Answer: Databento likely provides the needed historical option data, and the local QQQ OPRA files validate structurally for key option fields. That still does not prove SAFE-FAST field mapping, execution quality, trade outcome, or profitability.

Question: What happens if a backtest fails?

Answer: Diagnose and repair the failing layer. Do not call the project dead.

Question: What happens if one setup type fails?

Answer: Fix, narrow, replace, or remove that setup type from the profitable plan. The project continues.

Question: What happens if data is missing?

Answer: Identify the field, source it, change the proof path, or adjust the plan honestly.

Question: What protects future chats from wasting time?

Answer: Current baseline, exact fixed items, exact blockers, next single action, and repo proof file.

## Standing Clarifications

The project is not allowed to be called dead. Weak, failed, unclear, missing, or unprofitable results are diagnosis-and-repair triggers.

The goal remains a profitable trading plan. Profitability is not claimed until evidence proves it.

Do not prove a chart idea. Prove a fully defined trade plan.

Missing evidence is a blocker, not low confidence.

Use direct answers first. If the real answer is yes, no, or I do not know, say that plainly.
