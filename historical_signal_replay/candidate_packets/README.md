# SAFE-FAST Candidate Packets

Candidate packets are short, current summaries for active candidates. Use them to avoid repeating old discovery.

## How To Use

- Read `SAFE_FAST_BUILD_STATE.md` first.
- Read `SAFE_FAST_PROJECT_DASHBOARD.md` second.
- Read `SAFE_FAST_PROJECT_RULE_INDEX.md` third.
- Then read the relevant candidate packet before editing docs, rules, fixtures, calculators, or tests for that candidate.
- Treat packet facts as a navigation summary, not as proof or readiness.

## Packet Rules

- Do not fill evidence from a packet alone.
- Do not choose trades from a packet alone.
- Do not calculate P&L from a packet alone.
- Do not claim proof, profitability, readiness, or intake status from a packet alone.
- Update a packet when accepted docs, fixtures, rules, tests, or status change.
